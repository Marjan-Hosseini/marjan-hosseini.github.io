Short Biography

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at the University of Connecticut (UConn). I received my master's degree in computer science and engineering from Politecnico di Milano (PoliMi). I worked as a research fellow at DEpendable Evolvable Pervasive Software Engineering group at PoliMi on feature selection and optimization in cloud computing topics. My research interests lie in Computational Biology, Bayesian ML, Natural Language Processing (NLP), and Conversational AI.
